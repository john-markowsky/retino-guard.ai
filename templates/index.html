Why RetinoGuardAI?

RetinoGuardAI is a medical decision support system that utilizes AI technology to provide an efficient and accurate way of diagnosing diabetic retinopathy, a common retinal disease that can lead to severe vision loss or even blindness if left untreated. By analyzing retinal images, RetinoGuardAI can help optometrists and ophthalmologists detect and treat diabetic retinopathy early on, potentially saving patients' vision.
